Who am I ?

I am a young web developper. But first of all I'm a lover of literature. Few months ago, I got the idea to create a website where people could share their passion of writing and reading. My wish is to see some stories, any kind of them, evolving with many contributors. I explain myself : Everyone have the possibility to create a project, and write few paragraphers to introduce his idea, the story. Everyone, depending of his skills, have the possibility to write after. It is necessary to read what has been written above and thus continue the story as coherently as possible. The ultimate goal is to bring out stories that make sense without contradictions. There will obviously be different categories of stories. It would be a real personal feat if a community is created around my idea, if real stories emerge.
